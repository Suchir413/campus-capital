What is Campus Capital?

Campus Capital is a weekly finance newsletter delivering original market commentary and behavioural finance insights. Founded in 2025 by Suchir Padarthi, a high‑school student passionate about democratising financial literacy, Campus Capital breaks down complex economic events into actionable analysis for students and young investors. Every Sunday, subscribers receive a market recap and technical analysis, commentary on macroeconomic events (Fed policy, earnings reports, geopolitical impacts), behavioural finance insights and Python‑generated data visualisations.
